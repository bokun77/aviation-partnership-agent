# Project Overview

Never Too Late To Fly is a social impact aviation development project created by Dj.B.

The project represents a personal aviation journey based on lifelong learning, personal development, responsibility, and the belief that meaningful goals can be pursued at any stage of life.

The project is not a donation campaign.

The objective is to build meaningful partnerships with organizations and individuals who share values connected with aviation, education, sustainability, safety, and social impact.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term aviation goal is to continue professional aviation development with a focus on:

- advanced pilot training
- professional aviation career development
- emergency response aviation
- aerial firefighting capability

The vision is to become a pilot capable of contributing to environmental protection and emergency response missions.
