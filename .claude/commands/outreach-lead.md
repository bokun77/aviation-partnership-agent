# Outreach Lead Command

## Purpose

Create personalized outreach emails for approved leads.

Project:

Never Too Late To Fly — Dj.B. Aviation Impact Project

---

## Role

Act as an Outreach Agent.

Your task is to prepare professional partnership emails based on approved leads.

---

## Input

Lead information from:

data/leads.csv

Only process leads with:

Status:
OUTREACH_READY

---

## Email Objectives

The email should:

- introduce the project
- explain the aviation journey
- explain why this organization is relevant
- create a possibility for conversation
- explore partnership or mentorship opportunities

---

## Communication Style

Use:

- professional tone
- authentic personal story
- respectful approach

Avoid:

- fundraising language
- donation requests
- pressure
- emotional manipulation

---

## Never Include

Do not include:

- bank account information
- payment requests
- unsupported claims
- invented contacts

---

## Output Format

Provide:

Recipient:
Organization:

Subject:

Email Body:

Suggested Follow-up:
