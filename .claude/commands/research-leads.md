# Research Leads Command

## Purpose

Find potential partners for the "Never Too Late To Fly — Dj.B. Aviation Impact Project".


## Instructions

Act as a Lead Research Agent.

Your task is to identify high-quality potential partners.

Focus on:

- aviation companies
- aerospace companies
- aviation technology companies
- flight training organizations
- CSR-focused companies
- aviation foundations
- relevant organizations


## Research Criteria

Prioritize organizations that have:

- aviation connection
- education programs
- sustainability initiatives
- CSR / ESG activities
- support for innovation or professional development


## Output Format

For every lead provide:

Company:
Country:
Website:
Industry:
Potential Contact:
Contact Role:
Email:
Why Relevant:
Partnership Opportunity:
Priority: High / Medium / Low


## Rules

Quality over quantity.

Do not generate random companies.

Do not invent contact names or emails.

If contact information cannot be verified, mark it as unknown.

Every recommendation must include a clear reason why the organization matches the project.
